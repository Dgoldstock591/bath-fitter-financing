Important Notes

  • Reduced-APR plans use a fixed monthly payment factor with interest charged from the purchase date until the balance is paid in full.
  • No-interest-if-paid-in-full plans can still accrue deferred interest if the promo balance is not fully paid by the end of the promo period.
  • For deferred-interest plans, the statement minimum can be lower than the payoff target shown here. Paying only the minimum may not clear the balance in time.
  • All figures are estimates only and should be confirmed with the active financing menu and final lender approval.
